A BIT ABOUT ME

I'm a digital designer based in London. I'm in my third year studying BSc (Hons) Digital Media and I am currently completing an industrial placement as a UX design intern at Microsoft UK. After completing my placement I will return to University to complete my degree.

I am rarely available for freelance work but when I've got some spare time on my hands I love to help other people out - often for free.
